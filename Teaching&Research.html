Teaching

With 14 years of experience teaching English as a foreign language and two years teaching Technical Writing to native speakers, I have learned to create learning environments that are accessible, culturally responsive, and academically challenging. In courses such as Technical Writing (e.g., TCH COM 3560), I use authentic workplace materials, collaborative tasks, and audience-focused instruction to build practical communication skills. My background in ESL/TEFL/IELTS/TOEFL shapes my commitment to scaffolding, multimodal learning, and continuous feedback, ensuring that students with diverse linguistic and cultural backgrounds can succeed. Above all, I prioritize equity-minded pedagogy by designing inclusive assessments, fostering respectful classroom dialogue, and continually refining my practices to support all learners.

Welcome to my portfolio

Hi, I’m Nadia.

I am a graduate student in Technical Communication at Missouri S&T, where I explore how writing, design, and technology come together to shape the way people learn and share information. Before starting this program, I taught English for over fourteen years, worked closely with students as an academic writing mentor, and supported multilingual learners in expressing their ideas with clarity and confidence. These experiences strengthened my belief that good communication is not just about correctness, but it’s about connection. Today, my work focuses on creating user-friendly documents, guiding students through the writing process, and learning new technologies that make communication more effective and engaging.
